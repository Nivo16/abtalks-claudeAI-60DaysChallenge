# Day 3: Role-Based Prompting

## Objective
To explore how assigning different personas to Claude significantly changes the quality, perspective, and usefulness of its responses.

## Resources Reviewed

You are an AI educator teaching complete beginners.
Role-Based Prompting is a simple but powerful technique where you tell the AI what role or persona it should act as before asking your question.
## What is Role-Based Prompting?
It’s when you start your prompt with something like:
“You are a [specific expert/role]...”
This gives the AI a clear identity to respond from.
Why does it matter when using AI tools like Claude?
AI models like Claude are trained on massive amounts of data. Without guidance, they give average, generic answers. When you assign a role, Claude focuses on the knowledge, tone, and thinking style of that expert — making the answer much more useful and high-quality.
##  How does assigning a role change the quality?
Without a role → The AI tries to be helpful to everyone → shallow, safe, and generic answers.
With a role → The AI thinks and speaks like a real specialist → deeper insights, better<img width="784" height="1168" alt="dZQ8M" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/da75cd19-054f-47e3-a82c-d688a8d74d34" />
 structure, more practical advice.

## Example Without Role:
Prompt:
How should I price my new AI tool?
Response:
You can use cost-plus pricing or value-based pricing...
## Example With Role:
Prompt:
You are a successful SaaS founder who has sold two AI companies. You are practical and data-driven.
How should I price my new AI tool?
Response:
As someone who has done this twice, here’s what I recommend...
## Three Practical Benefits of Role-Based Prompting:
More Relevant Answers – You get advice tailored to your actual need.
Higher Expertise Level – The AI pulls from deeper, more specialized knowledge.
Better Structure & Tone – Responses become more professional, actionable, and easier to follow.
